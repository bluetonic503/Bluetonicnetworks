Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started as a freelancer?
Simply click "Get Started - It's Free" on our homepage, create your profile, add your skills and portfolio, and start browsing projects. Our onboarding process takes less than 10 minutes.
What are the fees for clients?
We charge a 10% service fee on all projects for clients. This includes access to our vetted talent pool, payment protection, and dedicated support.
How does payment protection work?
We use escrow payments for all projects. Clients deposit funds before work begins, and funds are released to freelancers only when milestones are completed and approved.
What kind of support do you offer?
We offer 24/7 email support, live chat during business hours, and dedicated account managers for enterprise clients. Our support team helps with hiring, disputes, and technical issues.
